ABOUT

I am a Finance Analytics researcher at King's College London, with a background in Accounting and Finance from SOAS University of London.

My research focuses on the intersection of quantitative finance, cybersecurity, and financial modeling. I explore emerging threats in financial systems, machine learning applications in risk assessment, and the security implications of modern economic infrastructure.

This site serves as a repository for my academic work, ongoing research projects, and published findings.
