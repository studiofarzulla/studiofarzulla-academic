Mission

Farzulla Research conducts independent research at the intersection of quantitative finance, cybersecurity, and artificial intelligence. We build working systems that demonstrate novel approaches to autonomous security, financial modeling, and machine learning, emphasizing reproducibility, open source tooling, and practical implementation.

Research Focus Areas

Adversarial AI Systems

Multi-agent security frameworks, autonomous red team/blue team competitions

Quantitative Finance

Market microstructure analysis, regulatory event studies, volatility modeling

Applied Machine Learning

Local LLM deployment, RAG systems, semantic supervision methodologies

Financial Crime Detection

OSINT-based fraud analysis, regulatory compliance mechanisms

Research Infrastructure

Kubernetes-native compute clusters, distributed systems for AI research

Organizational Philosophy

Build First: Working prototypes demonstrate feasibility before formal publication

Open Source: Code, methodologies, and datasets released publicly where possible

Independence: Research direction determined by interesting problems, not funding requirements

Reproducibility: Documentation first approach ensures replicability
